Booking Analytics Dashboard

Accommodation Bookings

Hotels account for 10% of total accommodation bookings, while motels make up 23%. Villas contribute 12%, and resorts add another 15%. Together, these categories represent the full accommodation segment of our platform, showing strong demand across diverse lodging options.

Travel Bookings

Train bookings represent 8% of travel reservations, flights contribute 14%, cabs stand at 6%, buses at 5%, and cruise ships at 3%. This distribution highlights the varied preferences of travelers, with flights and trains leading the segment.

Tour Packages

Tour packages alone account for 4% of total bookings. While smaller in share compared to accommodation and travel, this category reflects bundled experiences that continue to attract customers seeking convenience and value.
